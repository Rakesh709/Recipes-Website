How to make Samosa

Samosa is one of the most popular snacks of North Indian cuisine. This deep-fried snack is something that can satiate your hunger pangs and make you happy instantly! This traditional snack recipe needs no introduction and tastes best when served with mint chutney and red chutney I like Samosa because it is very tasty.

Ingredients to make Samosa

  • 4 cup all pupose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 2 teaspoon carom seeds
  • salt as required
  • 2 cup peas
  • 2 inch ginger
  • black pepper as required
  • 2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 cup refined oil
  • 1 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 4 cup all purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoon ghee
  • 4 boiled potato
  • 2 green chilli
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoon red chilli powder

Steps to make Samosa

  1. Prepare the masala for potato filling

    😃

  2. Add spices and boiled potatoes and cook for a while

    😆

  3. Prepare the dough for the Samosa

    😍

  4. Roll the dough in small puris and cut into half

    😋

  5. Fill the semi-circle with potato filling and deep fry

    😤

Description of samosa

Samosa is in triangular shape with crispy out with brown layer /colour. Samosa is smell like crispy snacks with salty taste and full varity of spices. A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region.
